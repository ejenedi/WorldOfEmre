Boat Facts & Details

Kayak (Single)

Perfect for solo exploration and speed. Ideal for experienced paddlers or quiet water conditions. Lightweight and easy to maneuver.

Tandem Kayak (Double)

Great for couples, families, or beginners. Highly stable and allows for shared paddling effort. Capacity: 2 adults + 1 small child.

Raft

Our most stable and largest option for groups of 4-8 people. Excellent for families and carrying substantial gear. Suitable for all skill levels.
